library('CatterPlots')

Stack Overflow taught me how to plot a heart in R (perhaps they will be the subject of my next thank you note):

t=seq(0, 2*pi, by=0.1)
xhrt <- function(t) 16*sin(t)^3
yhrt <- function(t) 13*cos(t)-5*cos(2*t)-2*cos(3*t)-cos(4*t)
y=yhrt(t)
x=xhrt(t)
